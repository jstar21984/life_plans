# Life Plans - Privacy Policy

## Data Collection

**Life Plans does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.**

### What We Store Locally

All your data is stored **only on your device**:
- Tasks (title, description, due dates)
- Categories (name, colors)
- App preferences (theme settings)

This data is stored using:
- **SQLite** - Local database on your device
- **SharedPreferences** - Local app settings

### No Data Transmission

- No data is sent to any server
- No analytics or tracking
- No third-party services access your data
- No cloud sync (data stays on your device)

### Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:
- **Notifications** - To remind you about tasks (iOS/macOS)
- No other permissions are required

### Your Control

You can delete all app data at any time by:
- Uninstalling the app
- Clearing app data from your device settings
